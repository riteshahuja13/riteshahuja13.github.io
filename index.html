Biography

I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Southern California. My advisor is Prof. Cyrus Shahabi. I completed my Bachelors degree in Computer Science from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I also obtained a research-based M.Phil. in CS at HKUST in 2016, under the supervision of Prof. Dimitris Papadias.

My research interest is in differential privacy and statistical inference, with a focus on mobility data publishing, machine learning, data structures & indexing, and others. More information is available on my CV.
